Overview: Belgium gets lost in the mix. It's understandable that it could get lost in the mix given that it is sandwiched between Germany, France, and the Netherlands and is known for waffles, Smurfs, and a statue of a young child peeing. Yet, Belgium rewards with richer than expected scenery and less tourist traffic. You'll taste some of the best food in Europe, including the creamiest chocolates, the best beer, and the best French fries. Town squares in Belgium are adorned with lofty spires and warm-brick gables. Rich artworks extolling the virtues of everyday life are shown in its museums. Little Belgium delights with everything from hipster metropolitan areas to serene convents, vintage lace to high-powered European politics.
