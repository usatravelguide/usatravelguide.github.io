Japan


Language: Japanese

Currency: Japanese Yen

Attractions: Mount Fuji, Imperial Palace, and Senso-ji

Overview: Known as the 'Land of the Rising Sun' Japan has an impressive history of ancient culture, including the many samurai traditions it has. Tourists can visit many large cities in the country all by high speed bullet trains, faster than taking a car. There is a lot to explore than just visiting the capital city of Tokyo; you can visit Mount Fuji, a high rise mountain surronded by magnificent lakes set in Fuji, and other attractions such as the Ginza's shopping mecca, Akihabara Electric Town, and Ueno district's national museums. As Covid-19 restrictions have settled down, non-Japanese citizens can travel either with a negative PCR test or proof of vaccination.
