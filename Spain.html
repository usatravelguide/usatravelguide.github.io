Kingdom of Spain


Language: Spanish

Currency: Euro

Attractions: Palacio Real, Park Guell, and La Sagrada Familia

Visa Required: No

Overview: Spain is a country with a blend of classic and modern architecture. Spain conjures up a variety of images for visitors, including bullfights, enormous cathedrals, top-notch artwork, Muslim palaces, whitewashed villages, mouthwatering paella, sunny beaches, and a buzzing nightlife. All of those things are present, but the people and their distinctive way of life are where the country's true attraction lies. This nation makes its own beat among the heat, from the rousing group sardana dance in Barcelona to the searing rat-a-tat-tat of flamenco in Sevilla.
