Overview: Peru is a country in South America that offers a diverse range of attractions for travelers. The ancient ruins of Machu Picchu are a must-see, as well as the colonial architecture of cities such as Lima and Cusco. The Amazon rainforest is a unique natural wonder to explore, while the coastal desert region is home to the enigmatic Nazca Lines. Foodies will enjoy sampling the delicious and varied cuisine, from ceviche to guinea pig. Adventurers can hike the Andes Mountains or surf the waves in Mancora. Overall, Peru is a fascinating and welcoming destination with a rich cultural heritage and plenty of exciting experiences to offer.
