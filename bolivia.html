Plurinational State of Bolivia


Language: Spanish, and many other indigenous languages

Currency: Bolivian Boliviano

Attractions: Plaza Murillo, Reserva Nacional de Fauna Andina Eduardo Avaroa, and Puma Punku

Visa Required: Yes. To apply for Bolivia visa, you need to complete an application form attached with your recently-taken color photo, prepare a valid passport, and submit them along with other supporting documents to the Bolivian embassy or consulate which is in charge of your residence area. You may also be able to apply online through the Bolivian government's e-visa website.
