How to Obtain a US Passport

1.Fill out Form DS-11: This form can be found on the U.S. Department of State website or obtained in person at a passport agency or acceptance facility. You will need to provide personal information, such as your name, address, and date of birth.

2.Provide evidence of U.S. citizenship: This can include a previous passport, a birth certificate, a certificate of naturalization, or a certificate of citizenship.

3.Provide a passport photo: You will need to provide a recent, color passport photo that meets the requirements outlined on the Department of State's website.

4.Pay the fee: The fees for a U.S. passport vary depending on the type of passport and processing time. You can find the current fees on the Department of State's website.

5.Submit your application: You can submit your application in person at a passport acceptance facility, such as a post office, clerk of court, or library. If you need your passport quickly, you can make an appointment at a regional passport agency.

6.Wait for your passport: Once your application has been processed, you should receive your U.S. passport within 4-6 weeks for routine processing or 2-3 weeks for expedited processing.
