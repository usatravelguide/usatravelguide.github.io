Taiwan (officially the Republic of China)


Language: Mandarin Chinese

Currency: New Taiwan dollar

Attractions: National Palace Museum, Lungshan Temple, and Yushan National Park

Visa Required: No

Overview:Taiwan, a country located off the coast of southeastern China, consists of over 400,000 tourists per year. Although the majority of the population is Chinese, the legacy of Japanese colonial authority still permeates much of Taiwanese society, and for the truly adventurous tourist, there is still indigenous Austronesian culture to discover. You can visit a great ton of national parks, shopping centers, and famous palaces and museums where you wish to explore Chinese culture more. Covid-19 still remains a trend in Taiwan, where you may be required to quarantine for at least 5 days upon arrival. If you plan on visiting Taiwan, go ahead! You can go around and explore what this island has to offer for their stunning nature!
