Republic of Korea


Language: Korean

Currency: South Korean won

Attractions: Namsan Seoul Tower, Gyeongbokgung Palace, and National Museum of Korea

Overview:South Korea has emerged from the shadows of its turbulent past and cemented its place as one of the world's major economic powers. Since the turn of the century, South Korean pop culture has become enormously popular all over East Asia, making it a very popular tourist destination. Many of which include many popular festivals in the country, including the Jeju Fire, Jinhae Cherry Blossom, and Lotus Lantern Festiva, symbolizing Korean culture. Be sure to also check out the DMZ, where you can view North Korea from South Korea.
