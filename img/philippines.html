Republic of the Philippines


Language: Filipino

Currency: Philippine peso

Attractions: Fort Santiago, National Museum of Fine Arts, and Chocolate Hills

Visa Required: No

Overview: Beautiful beaches, laid-back island atmosphere, an intriguing history, and some of the world's most genuinely nice people can all be found in the Philippines. It's among the best places I've ever gone. In fact, people are eager to return to the Philippines because it is such a beautiful place.
