Socialist Republic of Vietnam


Language: Vietnamese

Currency: Vietnamese dong

Attractions: Cu Chi Tunnel, Temple of Literature, and Ha Long Bay

Visa Required: eVisa

Overview:Located in Southeast Asia surronded by the South China Sea, Vietnam is known for its staggering natural beauty and cultural entanglement. Here you can visit gorgeous landscapes, try out some tasty food, or visit beautiful caves. When visiting, expect a lot of heat and humidity as the climate tends to be mild tropical or subtropical. While Vietnam is also communist and our former enemy during the Vietnam war, it is generally safe to visit as the US recognizes the new country. From visiting tropical beaches to exploring national parks, it is highly reccommended to visit this magnificent country!
