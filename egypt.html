Arab Republic of Egypt


Language: Arabic

Currency: Egyptian Pound

Attractions: The Great Pyramid of Giza, Ras Mohammed Nature Reserve, and The Egyptian Museum in Cairo

Visa Required: Yes. To apply for Egypt e-visa, you can visit the official website of the Egyptian e-Visa Portal and fill out the application form. You will need to provide personal information, passport details, and travel plans, as well as pay the visa fee. Once your application is approved, you will receive your e-Visa via email, which you should print and bring with you when traveling to Egypt.
