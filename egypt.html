Arab Republic of Egypt


Language: Arabic

Currency: Egyptian Pound

Attractions: The Great Pyramid of Giza, Ras Mohammed Nature Reserve, and The Egyptian Museum in Cairo

Visa Required: Yes. To apply for Egypt e-visa, you can visit the official website of the Egyptian e-Visa Portal and fill out the application form. You will need to provide personal information, passport details, and travel plans, as well as pay the visa fee. Once your application is approved, you will receive your e-Visa via email, which you should print and bring with you when traveling to Egypt.

Overview: Egypt is a captivating travel destination, boasting world-renowned historical landmarks, stunning natural beauty, and a vibrant culture. The country is home to the iconic pyramids of Giza, the ancient temples of Luxor and Karnak, and the Valley of the Kings, which together provide a fascinating glimpse into Egypt's rich history and architecture. Visitors can also enjoy the Red Sea's crystal-clear waters, explore the Sahara Desert, or cruise down the Nile River. With a warm and welcoming people, delicious cuisine, and a bustling atmosphere, Egypt is an unforgettable travel experience.
