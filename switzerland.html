Overview: Discovering the numerous mountain communities in Switzerland, a country renowned for its natural beauty, is a must for tourists. One of the most beautiful mountain ranges in the world, the Swiss Alps cover 60% of the country. Numerous outdoor activities are available, including some of the top hiking trails in the world. The cities are as as stunning as the surrounding scenery, and Zermatt is the ideal location at the base of the famed Matterhorn. Geneva is renowned for its arts and culture. Lugano is a popular lakeside vacation.
