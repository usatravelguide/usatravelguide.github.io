Overview: Fiji is an archipelago in the South Pacific, known for its stunning natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and friendly locals. Visitors can relax on white sand beaches, swim in turquoise lagoons, and explore lush rainforests. Must-see attractions include the Coral Coast, where you can snorkel and dive in the Great Astrolabe Reef, and the Yasawa Islands, a chain of 20 volcanic islands with pristine beaches and excellent hiking. In addition, cultural experiences such as traditional Fijian village visits and a Kava ceremony provide insight into the country's rich history and customs. Don't miss the opportunity to try Fijian cuisine, which combines Indian, Chinese, and Pacific Island influences.
