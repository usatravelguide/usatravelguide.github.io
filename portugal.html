Portugese Republic


Language: Portugese

Currency: Euro

Attractions: Mosterio dos Jeronimos, Torre de Belem, and The National Gallery

Visa Required: No

Overview: Portugal has the ideal blend of rocky coastlines, white sand beaches, architecturally stunning cities, and a thriving food scene. Oh, and a lot of wine, of course. Portugal is a fantastic retreat for single female travelers because it's also among the most reasonably priced and safe overseas places.
